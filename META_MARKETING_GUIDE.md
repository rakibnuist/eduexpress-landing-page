## ðŸŽ¯ Phase 2: Campaign Strategy (The "2-Step Funnel")

Since you are a new brand without a huge reputation yet, we will use a **Filter & Close** strategy.

### Campaign A: The "Hook" (Cold Audience)
*   **Goal:** Get cheap traffic to the landing page and let the "No IELTS" hook filter them.
*   **Objective:** **Traffic** (Optimization: Landing Page Views) OR **Leads** (Optimization: Performance goals). *Recommended: Leads (Website Conversion).*
*   **Budget:** 60% of total budget.

### Campaign B: The "Reminder" (Retargeting)
*   **Goal:** Convert people who visited but didn't apply.
*   **Objective:** **Sales / Leads** (Optimization: `CompleteRegistration` Event).
*   **Audience:** Custom Audience (Website Visitors - Last 30 Days).
*   **Budget:** 40% of total budget.

## ðŸ“Š Phase 5: Optimization (How to Win)

Check your Ads Manager every morning.

| Metric | Benchmark | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CTR (Link Click-Through)** | < 1.0% | **Bad.** Change your Ad Image immediately. Stop the ad. |
| **CTR** | > 1.5% | **Good.** Keep running. |
| **CPC (Cost Per Click)** | > $0.10 | **Expensive.** Your audience is too small or bored. broaden targeting. |
| **Landing Page Views** | Low | If Clicks are high but Views are low, your site is slow. (We fixed this with Vercel/Next.js). |
| **Results (Registrations)** | < $2.00 | **Excellent.** Scale budget by 20%. |
